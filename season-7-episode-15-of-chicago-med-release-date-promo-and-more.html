    Season 7, Episode 15 Of Chicago Med: Release Date, Promo And More

    Chicago Med, NBCs acclaimed medical drama, is back for another episode, as are the other One Chicago series.     30 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Chicago Med, NBC’s acclaimed medical drama, is back for another episode, as are the other One Chicago series. This week’s episode will focus on a challenging case involving Dr. Dylan Scott’s patient, Guy Lockard. The show’s next attack, Things Meant to Be Bent Not Broken, will debut on March 9, 2022.

    Several additional instances will be intertwined with this one, similar to the previous week’s episode, All the Things That Could Have Been. After a series of delays due to various factors, the One Chicago events are finally back on track, and it appears that this will be the case for some time.

    Continue reading for more information about Chicago Med season 7, episode 15.

    Chicago Med promotion: A drug dealer’s complex case?

    In this week’s episode, Dr. Scott’s patient is a drug dealer. This creates a moral dilemma for the qualified doctor, who must choose between his medical and civilian duties.

    Several other examples will also be highlighted, including one in which a woman fears she is infested with parasites. This case will be handled by Dr. Daniel Charles, played by Oliver Platt, and Dr. Vanessa Taylor, played by Ashja Cooper. The short promo contains a lot of scenes, implying a jam-packed episode.

    According to the episode’s official summary,

    “Scott feels his patient is a drug dealer; Halstead must decide how to spend his whistleblower payment; Charles and Taylor assist a patient who believes she is parasite-infested; and Marcel and Blake are partnered with an egotistical physician.”

    When Dr. Crockett Marcel, played by Dominic Rains, and Dr. Blake, played by Sarah Rafferty, are teamed with an arrogant surgeon whose name has not yet been disclosed, the promo also hints at danger for them.

    When will the latest episode of Chicago Med air?

    The latest episode of NBC’s hit medical drama will play on the NBC channel on Wednesday, March 9, 2022, at 7 p.m. ET. It is also available to watch on Peacock’s streaming platforms, where you can discover all prior episodes of the show. More information will be provided as it becomes available.
