    WWE RAW Tickets Already Flying For New Years Day Episode

    WWE usually likes to bring special moments to episodes of RAW and SmackDown that fall on certain days. They have an opportunity to start 2024 with a bang on the red brand, and fans are ready in San Diego.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    WWE usually likes to bring special moments to episodes of RAW and SmackDown that fall on certain days. They have an opportunity to start 2024 with a bang on the red brand, and fans are ready in San Diego.

    WWE Monday Night Raw is scheduled to take place on Monday, January 1st, 2024, at the Pechanga Arena San Diego in San Diego, CA. That will mark a New Year’s Day episode for the red brand.

    Wrestle Tix reports that there are 763 available tickets for the event, with the current setup accommodating 9,144 attendees. The total number of tickets distributed for the show stands at 8,381, which is an increase of 811 in the last week.

    WWE Monday Night Raw

    MON JAN 01, 2024 – 4:30 PM

    Pechanga Arena San Diego, San Diego CA

    Available Tickets => 763

    Current Setup => 9,144

    Tickets Distributed => 8,381 (+811 since the last update: 7 days ago)

    New upper decks added which is a weekly trend for this show.

    Last time here:

    7/4/2022 => Raw => 6,121

    It’s worth noting that the addition of new upper decks to the arena is a recurring trend for this weekly show. The last time WWE Raw visited the Pechanga Arena San Diego was on July 4th, 2022, when it drew a crowd of 6,121 spectators.

    We will have to see what happens on the January 1st episode of WWE RAW, but there could be a lot of possibilities. The road to the Royal Rumble will be in full upswing as well, with the event slated to take over Tropicana Field on January 27th.
