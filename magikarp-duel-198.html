    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Magikarp (Japanese: コイキング Koiking) is a figure in Pokémon Duel. It was made available in version 3.0.1. Shiny Magikarp was made available in version 4.0.7 and added exclusively to Time Booster (White) and Time Booster (Blue) in version 6.2.9.

    Release information

    Magikarp

    This figure was available as a Reward for clearing Challenge 4 in the Training Center starting in version 5.0.10. It was also available in Gym Boosters during the Round 1 Flying Gym Cup held from February 9 to 16, 2017 and as a Duel Wins Reward and in Gym Boosters during the Round 1 Grass Gym Cup held from April 19 to 26, 2017. It was also available as a Monthly Points Reward from June 12 to July 3, 2019.

    Shiny Magikarp

    This figure was available as an Achievement Reward during the Rowlet, Litten, and Popplio, Scizor, Empoleon, and Lucario, and Zekrom, Kyurem, and Reshiram Team Matches held from November 24 to 27, 2017, December 22 to 25, 2017, and April 20 to 23, 2018, respectively. It was also available as a Reward from the October 9 to 11, 2018 Dev Showdown.

    Origin

    Splash and Flail are moves in the Pokémon games that Magikarp can learn. Hydro Vortex is a Z-Move in the games although Magikarp cannot use it.
