    As Porter Robinson helped bring this year’s HARD Summer to a close over the weekend, the forward thinking producer treated fans at the HARDer stage to some new renditions of his usual Worlds performance. Robinson dropped three new live edits into the set, offering up new takes on “Flicker,” “Natural Light,” and “Shepherdess,” and the results are as dazzling as you’d expect.

    Porter Robinson’s music is known for its ability to take the audience on an emotional journey, and the new live edits are no exception to that rule. On the “Flicker” edit, he mixes the familiar tune with Kanye West’s “The Glory,” while “Shepherdess” was mixed in with the recently unveiled “She Heals Everything.” All in all, the edits offer up a new take on the beloved material from Worlds, all set to a stunning visual show.

    If you missed out on Porter Robinson‘s performance at HARD Summer this year, you’re in luck, as the new live edits were captured by a fan and uploaded to Reddit for your enjoyment.
